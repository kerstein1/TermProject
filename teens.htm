First Bank Account

Most teens obtain their first debit and savings account from their parents/guardians. Especially, since most banks have an age limit to when you can open your first debit and savings account.

    2. Short Term Goals vs. Long-Term Goals: Saving for a bike vs. a book. It is going to take less time to save for a book than it is for a bike.

  2. Compound Interest:

    3. interest you earn on your interest (earning twice as much) This can happen if you start saving early.

  3. Building your emergency fund:

    4. Creating an account where you don't touch that money. You act like you don't have it. Instead it is earning interest.
