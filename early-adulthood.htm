Early Adulthood

    After Graduation:

There are a lot of things that happen when you graduate, such as finding the perfect job. The older you get the more responsibilities you have and the more money you spend. It is important to budget yourself and start to save, especially if you have a long-term goal to buy a house. It is important to plan how much you're going to save from every paycheck.

    Tips

  • Make sure to take advantage of your company's benefits:

  • 401K:You might not want to do it right now, but think of your future self. For instance, by contributing you can lower your tax burder and don't pay the taxes until you withdraw it in retirmenet. Some Companies evn contribute a certain % for every dollar you put in.

  • Look at Saving Account Options and CDs: Maybe start thinking about using your saving accounts as an emergency fund and a CD to save for a short-term goal or long-term goal depending on how much time you leave it.
