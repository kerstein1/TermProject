    Becoming Financially Independent

This is usually the time when college students venture out into the world and start figuring things out for themselves. One of the most exciting times in a persons' life, but also one of the scariest. Especially, if you have never talked about financial wellness before, the world can appear scary. Below are a few tips on how to open your first bank account as well as select where you want to start building your credit.

    Opening Your Own Bank Account

Opening your first bank account without the supervision of your parents is quite exciting. But usually one gets a bank account either because your parents use that bank or because its in close proximity of your school.

All of those reasons make a lot of sense, but there are other banks that have a lot of benefits that one might have never thought about.
